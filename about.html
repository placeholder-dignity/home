DIGnity is a university project created for the Interactive Transmedia Narrative for New Media class of the Politecnico di Milano.

DIGnity is an interactive visual novel which revolves around the topic of workaholism and over production, related to mental health and well-being. The plot follows the adventure of Lehns, a young explorer recruited by the Tower, who explores the Underground in search of precious gems on the government behalf.
